## Tips for remote AI/data applications

- "Remote" sometimes means "remote within a single country" — read the fine print.
- Some "remote" roles require US work authorization without sponsorship.
- Async-first companies expect strong written communication and design docs.
- Confirm time zone overlap requirements before applying.
