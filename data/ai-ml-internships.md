## Before applying

- Does the JD ask for Python, PyTorch, TensorFlow, NLP, computer vision, or statistics?
- Does your resume show projects that match those skills?
- Did you include the tools listed in the JD?
- Did you avoid adding fake skills?
- Did you confirm the role is still open?
