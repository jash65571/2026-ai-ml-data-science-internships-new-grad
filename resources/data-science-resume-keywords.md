## Strong resume bullet templates

- Designed an A/B test on [feature] that increased [metric] by [%].
- Built a [forecast/segmentation/scoring] model that drove [decision/result].
- Analyzed [dataset] to identify [insight], adopted by [team].
- Created a SQL/Looker/Tableau dashboard used by [team] to track [metric].

## Example bullets

- Designed an A/B test that increased onboarding completion by 7% (p<0.01, n=42k).
- Built a customer LTV model that informed marketing spend allocation across 4 channels.
- Analyzed 2 years of order data in BigQuery to surface 3 retention risks, prioritized by Product.
- Created a Looker dashboard tracking activation funnels; used weekly by the growth team.
